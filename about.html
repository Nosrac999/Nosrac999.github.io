About Carson Brough

Narrative


My name is Carson Brough and I am a student at Crimson Cliffs Highschool. I have been going to school since I was the age of 4. I have learned a lot through my years, and I am very grateful for it. School has taught me how to work hard and do things even when I don't want to do them. I currently hold a 4.0 GPA, and am a part of the High School Wrestling Team.


My Journey so Far

I have now been to 6 different schools, and have lived in two cities. I was born in West Jordan, Utah, and moved down to St. George when I was three years old. I have grown a lot since I moved here, and I very thankful for the opportunities I have had down here.
